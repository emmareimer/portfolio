The Why

Originally from Bloomington, IL, Emma moved to Denver in early 2017. With her background in sales, marketing, and property and business management, Emma joined a small tech company, Proximity, in 2019 whose focus was providing exceptional workplace management software. She started as the Community Manager and quickly moved to a Customer Success role. After working with the software for so long Emma decided she wanted to learn more about coding. Currently enrolled in Denver University's full-stack coding program, Emma is an aspiring full-stack developer. She likes seeing small parts come together to create something out of nothing. Emma's favorite part about coding is developing the logic for a new project and troubleshooting to watch that project come in to fruition. She believs that there is always something new to learn.
